Video Game Sales and Metacritic Scores

1. Introduction

The video game industry has experienced tremendous growth over the past few decades, evolving from simple 8-bit entertainment into a global market worth billions of dollars. With advancements in technology, storytelling, and graphics, video games have become one of the most influential forms of modern media1.

This project examines the relationship between video game sales and their corresponding Metacritic scores. Understanding how review scores correlate with commercial success may provide insights for developers, publishers, and marketers seeking to optimize product performance in an increasingly competitive marketplace2.
